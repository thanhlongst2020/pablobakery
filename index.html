About Us

The best desserts ever!

Pablo West Bakery is an emerging name in the food industry. We are situated in Kiambu and are founded by Helen Barbara. We have a team of baking experts, coming from diverse parts of the country, elevating the brand with an overwhelming passion for good desserts. Our team is our pride. Our top-selling products are Red Velvet Cupcakes.

We at Pablo West Bakery consider our clients' to be our powerhouse. We love to take and adapt to customer feedback, making us one of the friendliest bakers in the Kiambu neighborhood. Since 2020, we have serviced over 790 and hope to make more souls happy. Our aim is to be the number one dessert supplier in the country
